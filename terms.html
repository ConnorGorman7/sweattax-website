2. How SweatTax Works

SweatTax is a workout accountability tool. You schedule workouts and set a monetary stake per missed session. If you fail to check in within the allowed window, a charge equal to your chosen stake is recorded. After 3 unpaid missed sessions, you are temporarily blocked from scheduling new workouts until your outstanding balance is paid.

  • Stake amount: $1–$10 per miss, chosen by you
  • Check-in window: begins at your scheduled start time, with a 15-minute grace period
  • Gym check-in: requires GPS verification within the gym's geofence
  • At-home check-in: requires a minimum 25-minute active timer session
